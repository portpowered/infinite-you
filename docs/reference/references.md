## 📖 References

Use these references when you want to build a new factory. 

The general lesson you'll need is that basically, real life is random by default. Randomness is bad because sometimes it means things fail, then you have to deal with it by spending more money. Less random is good, because it gives you quality, cost, and throughput. By extension, AI agents are random so treat them in equivalence to random workers on the factory line.

The below are general theories, concepts, and methods for understanding and dealing with randomness.
